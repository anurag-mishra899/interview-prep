These naming proposals are designed for:

## TIER 1 RECOMMENDATIONS (Top 3)

### 1. **SPECTRA™** Framework
**Full Name:** Specification-Centric Transformation & Regenerative Architecture

**Tagline:** "Make your specifications the asset, not the code"

**Rationale:**
- **SPECTRA** evokes "spectrum" (completeness, 7 dimensions) and "spec" (specification)
- Sounds sophisticated and scientific
- Short, memorable, globally pronounceable
- No direct competitors in enterprise frameworks
- Works across industries: manufacturing (product specs), construction (blueprints), pharma (compound specs)

**Trademark Viability:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (Very High - unique in this domain)

**Visual Identity Potential:**
- Logo: Spectrum/prism showing 7 colors → 7 dimensions
- Brand colors: Already established (blue, cyan, amber spectrum)

**Positioning Statement:**
> "SPECTRA is the industry-leading framework for transforming specifications into strategic assets in the age of generative AI. Used by Fortune 500 companies to achieve 10x faster delivery with 70% fewer defects."

## BRAND PROTECTION STRATEGY

### Defensive Registrations
To protect the brand, also register:
- Common misspellings (e.g., "Spectre" if using SPECTRA)
- Acronym variations (e.g., "SPC Framework")
- Plural/singular forms
- Social media handles (@spectraframework, @7dspec, etc.)

### Brand Usage Guidelines
Create a brand book covering:
1. Proper usage: "the SPECTRA™ Framework" (not "spectra" or "Spectra framework")
2. Logo specifications
3. Color palette enforcement
4. Prohibited uses
5. Partner/licensee guidelines
