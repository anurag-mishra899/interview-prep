# Product Manager - AI Products

## About the Role

We are seeking an exceptional Product Manager to lead the development and evolution of our AI-powered products. This role sits at the intersection of cutting-edge AI technology and transformative user experiences. You'll work alongside world-class researchers, engineers, designers, and cross-functional teams to build products that harness the power of large language models (LLMs), machine learning, and AI agents to solve complex problems and create value for millions of users.

As our AI Product Manager, you will be responsible for driving product vision, strategy, and execution across the full product lifecycle—from conception through launch and continuous optimization. You'll operate in a fast-paced, highly ambiguous environment where you'll need to balance strategic thinking with hands-on execution, technical depth with user empathy, and innovation with responsible AI development.

You'll be expected to leverage AI tools to multiply your own productivity while simultaneously building AI products for our users. This unique position requires both the ability to work effectively with AI systems and to translate complex AI capabilities into intuitive, valuable product experiences.

---

## What You'll Do

### Strategic Leadership & Vision
- Define and drive multi-year product vision, strategy, and roadmap aligned with company objectives and AI technological evolution
- Conduct competitive analysis, market research, and trend monitoring to identify opportunities and threats in the rapidly evolving AI landscape
- Build compelling business cases with ROI modeling, revenue projections, and resource allocation recommendations
- Establish quarterly and annual objectives and key results (OKRs) that drive measurable impact
- Identify and evaluate strategic partnership, integration, and M&A opportunities
- Provide strategic oversight and governance for AI product development, establishing guardrails and principles for responsible AI deployment

### Product Development & Execution
- Lead end-to-end product development from ideation to launch for new AI-powered features and products, with strong focus on 0-to-1 creation
- Translate complex AI capabilities into clear product requirements, user stories, and technical specifications
- Prioritize features and initiatives using data-driven frameworks (RICE, KANO, ICE) while balancing user needs, business impact, and technical feasibility
- Partner with AI researchers, ML engineers, data scientists, designers, policy experts, and go-to-market teams to deliver exceptional products
- Lead sprint planning, standups, retrospectives, and facilitate rapid iteration cycles in an Agile environment
- Coordinate product launches across engineering, marketing, sales, support, and legal teams
- Resolve ambiguous and novel product situations requiring complex judgment and decision-making

### AI Product Innovation
- Design and orchestrate multi-agent AI workflows to automate and accelerate product development processes
- Develop sophisticated prompts, evaluate model outputs, and work directly with AI systems to enhance product capabilities
- Integrate AI tools and platforms into product workflows, establishing quality frameworks and validation processes
- Leverage AI for research synthesis, competitive analysis, data analysis, documentation, and stakeholder communication
- Collaborate with research teams to translate AI research into product features and identify high-potential use cases grounded in customer needs
- Define model behaviors, character, and reinforcement signals that shape how users experience AI products

### Data-Driven Decision Making & Analytics
- Define and track product success metrics, model performance indicators (precision, recall, latency, bias detection), and business KPIs
- Design, execute, and analyze A/B tests and experiments to validate hypotheses and optimize product experiences
- Monitor dashboards, detect anomalies, investigate root causes, and extract actionable insights from user behavior data
- Track model drift, performance degradation, and bias; coordinate with ML teams on retraining and optimization strategies
- Utilize SQL and analytics tools to query data, build reports, and inform product decisions

### Cross-Functional Leadership & Communication
- Build consensus and alignment across diverse stakeholder groups including technical teams, executives, legal, policy, and business partners
- Present complex AI concepts and product strategies to both technical and non-technical audiences
- Mentor junior PMs and contribute to building product culture and best practices
- Navigate organizational complexity and influence without direct authority
